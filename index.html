About me

Hello! Thanks for dropping by! My name is Johnathon Lu. I'm currently a Computer Engineer studying at San Jose State University. I made this website primarily for my resume, but also to give a glimpse of my life outside of study and work that can't be expressed well on paper and also to track my progress on projects I'm working on. A little bit about my interests, I am a home cook and love cooking for myself and learning new dishes. It started as just a way to save money in college and to get away from the monotony of university meals, but, recently, has become a form of expression for me. Speaking of form of expressions, I am also a pianist. I have been playing the piano ever since I was younger, and while I don't play with competitions and recitals in mind anymore, I do enjoy sitting on the bench and learning a new piece. Currently, I mainly play at home in my room after finishing up work.
